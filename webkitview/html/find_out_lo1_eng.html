Monthly cost of food for children, included in prices are 38.85 euros per month. Cost of materials and services for the child, included in price for the programs are 55 per month per child included in the daily programs, and 40 euros per child in half-day programs. Parents of children for whom the Municipality of Ljubljana is obligated to cover part of the costs of the program is further reduced for the payment in the first age group for 20% in the 2nd till 4th income group; 14% for the 5th till 9th income group. In the second age group it is reduced for 10% in the 2nd till 4th income group, and 7% for the 5th to 9th income group. By 2005, the Municipality of Ljubljana recorded a 65-68% enrollment of children in kindergartens, in 2011 it reached 85.1% enrollment.
