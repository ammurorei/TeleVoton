The proposed follow the guidelines adopted by the policy of Slovenia in the field of taxation, which is directed to reducing the tax burden of administrative economy, paying particular attention to the micro (one-person) businesses and entrepreneurs. The directive from 2010 pursued goals of simplifying, modernizing and harmonizing the rules on invoicing and harmonization of rules concerning VAT in the transactions within the EU: set the same rules for supplies and acquisitions of goods within the EU, followed by the rules for issuing an invoice. More specific rules for issuing are applicable an invoice, the removal of obstacles to issue a digital invoicing, introduction of simplified accounts in certain cases when issuing invoices between taxpayers.
