The regulation of resources for the operation of the Public Institute of Ljubljana Puppet Theatre do not provide the basis for market oriented activities, it is necessary to specifically redefine marketing activity. The basic change is an indication for the possibility of market based activity, but in accordance with the statute, meaning that it does not compromise the provision of public services. In addition, it is also a reference to an expanded set of potential activities that may be carried out by the institution, which is also a consequence of the acquisition of a new multipurpose hall Stage Under the Stars. The Act #33 will be also actualizes to the new situation. Since the year 2011 the entire building of the theater is in the management of the public institution Ljubljana Puppet Theatre.
